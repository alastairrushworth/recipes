## Brownies

### Ingredients

- 120g dark chocolate
- 60g milk
- 60g butter
- 100g sugar
- 2 eggs
- 125g ground hazelnuts
- 45g cocoa powder
- 60g flour

### Method

1. Preheat the oven to 160C.
2. Melt butter and chocolate gently in a pan.
3. Whisk the eggs and sugar together to a sabayon.
4. Combine with the dry ingredients.
5. Bake for 20 minutes.
